## Cytognosis Foundation

This project is developed under the auspices of the [Cytognosis Foundation](https://cytognosis.org), a nonprofit organization dedicated to advancing AI-native technology for preventive healthcare and disease detection.

### Mission

The Cytognosis Foundation's mission is to develop accessible, scientifically rigorous tools that empower researchers and clinicians to improve human health through cellular diagnostics and precision medicine.

### Core Values

- **Scientific Rigor**: Evidence-based approaches with reproducible, peer-reviewed results
- **Healthcare Accessibility**: Open science principles and equitable access to technology
- **Data Privacy**: HIPAA-compliant, privacy-first design in all our tools
- **Collaboration**: Community-driven development and knowledge sharing
- **Innovation**: Cutting-edge AI/ML techniques applied to healthcare challenges
