## 2\. What I’m building / exploring

**Cytognosis** is a nonprofit initiative that maps and monitors cellular health states in real-time.

Our approach:

- Build **AI-native “digital organism” models** across cellular scales (e.g., Neuroverse for neurons, Immunoverse for immune cells).  
- Integrate multimodal data (cfRNA/DNA, proteomics, transcriptomics, metabolomics).  
- Enable proactive health monitoring by detecting deviations in molecular states before disease manifests.

**Early prototypes include:**

- AI Evaluation Benchmarks for cfRNA Diagnostic Panels.  
- Graph neural networks integrating protein–protein and gene–gene networks with patient data.  
- Explainability tools so clinicians and patients can interpret molecular health profiles.

---

## 3\. Why now?

- Multimodal biological datasets and AI architectures (foundation models, GNNs) are converging.  
- Healthcare systems remain reactive and fragmented, failing to leverage this data for prevention.  
- A nonprofit, open-science structure enables equitable global access—beyond what for-profit biotech typically prioritizes.

Now is the right time to pioneer AI for proactive, equitable health.
