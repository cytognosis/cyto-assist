# Astera Institute Residency Application

**Track 1: Neuroscience and Artificial Intelligence & Track 2: Life Sciences and Open Science**

## Project Summary (max 300 chars)

An open-source cellular intelligence platform ("GPS for Health") that maps brain health states along continuous biological axes, detecting pre-disease trajectory deviations years before symptoms emerge through privacy-preserving on-device causal AI.

## Technical Approach (max 1000 chars)

The "Neuroverse" platform replaces static, categorical disease labels with continuous molecular trajectories. Cytoverse (The Map) is an AI health coordinate system trained on single-cell multi-omic data (e.g., PsychENCODE, ROSMAP) that learns the high-dimensional State Space of the brain. Rather than treating disease as a distinct identity, we use pairwise disease geometry to map continuous axes of vulnerability. Cytoscope (The Sensor) operationalizes this by fusing conversational data with wearable physiology (EEG, fNIRS, HRV) into a continuous stream. Cytonome (The Navigator) processes this stream using purpose-built, on-device causal AI (<5mW). By utilizing dynamic representation learning and structural causal models, Cytonome accurately identifies clinically meaningful deviations from a personal baseline entirely at the edge, linking neural mechanisms and machine learning architectures to predict cognitive and psychiatric outcomes cleanly.

## Target Users (max 1000 chars)

1. **Academic & Clinical Researchers:** By releasing our data, embeddings, and frameworks under open-source licenses (Apache 2.0 / CC BY 4.0), we provide AI-ready, missing-data generation infrastructure for the neuroscience and computational biology communities.
2. **Health Systems & Public Health:** We remove vendor lock-in for under-resourced clinics, providing scalable, continuous monitoring software to track population-level disease emergence.
3. **Individuals:** We empower people with a private, on-device mental health compass that detects trajectory changes early, empowering autonomy over their health destiny without sharing sensitive psychiatric data to centralized corporate clouds.

## Scope during Residency (max 1000 chars)

**In-scope:** Building the Neuroverse Macro behavioral layer and integrating it with our Micro molecular layer. We will compile our first proprietary multimodal dataset from a Phase 0 internal pilot (conversation + wearable EEG/fNIRS). We will utilize Astera's compute (H100s) to fine-tune compact (3B) foundation models and sparse autoencoders that map physiological dimensions to Cytoverse coordinates, subsequently distilling them for edge deployment.
**Out of scope:** Large-scale commercial hardware manufacturing of new Cytoscope continuous sensors, and multi-center FDA-cleared clinical trials. The residency will culminate in a validated, open-source privacy-protocol, our Neuroverse model weights, and preliminary multimodal data ready for ARPA-H scale deployment.

## Open Science (max 1000 chars)

Open science is our operating system. All software, federated learning pipelines, and edge AI weights will be released under Apache 2.0. All documentation, methodologies, and anonymized aggregate data will be released under CC BY 4.0. We adhere strictly to the Allen Institute's standards for "truly open" AI: releasing not just weights, but full training code, data schemas, evaluation benchmarks, and failure logs. Building the Neuroverse transparently ensures it becomes resilient, trusted public infrastructure.
