## Initiatives & Sprints

### EVIDENT (Evidence-Based Validation and Innovation for Rapid Therapeutics in Behavioral Health)

- **Announced:** November 2025
- **Investment:** Up to **$100 million**
- **Awards:** Fixed at $4M each, 24-month performance period, milestone-based
- **Office:** PHO
- **Overlap Risk for Cytognosis:** **Medium-High**

**Scope:** Transform behavioral healthcare by developing objective, validated clinical endpoints for emerging therapies (neuroplastogens, neuromodulation, digital therapeutics).

**Technical Areas:**

1. **TA1: Objective Measurement of Clinical Change** — biomarkers for continuous behavioral health monitoring (neuroimaging, wearables, digital phenotyping) to detect neuroplastic changes
2. **TA2: Unobtrusive Data Collection** — multimodal data during treatment sessions, interpersonal dynamics
3. **TA3: Actionable Insights** — matching treatments, mitigating risks, monitoring progress
4. **TA4:** Rolling basis, details open-ended

**Data Repository:** Awardees must deliver de-identified data + biological samples to ARPA-H Rapid Response Data Repository.

## Detailed Overlap Analysis: Cytognosis vs. ARPA-H Programs

### PROSPR — **HIGH OVERLAP RISK** ⚠️

**Full name:** PROactive Solutions for Prolonging Resilience
**PM:** Andrew Brack, Ph.D. | **Office:** PHO | **Solicitation:** ARPA-H-SOL-24-115

**PROSPR scope:** Identify biochemical and physiological markers of aging, develop in-home data collection and clinical trial protocols that assess age-associated health outcomes in 3 years instead of decades, build a "new therapeutic industry" for interventions focused on maintaining health during aging.

**PROSPR awardees (active):**

| Performer | Focus | Approach |
| --------- | ----- | -------- |
| Stanford University | Healthspan scoring | Harmonizing datasets → "PROSPR-IC healthspan score," plus in-home digital health assessment tech |
| UT Health San Antonio | Drug repurposing | Repurposing FDA-approved drugs (SGLT2 inhibitors, rapamycin, semaglutide) for aging via phase 3 hybrid trials |
| Columbia University | Biomarker identification | Analyzing multiple trial datasets to find biomarkers responsive to aging interventions |
| Apollo Alpha | Compound testing | Energy homeostasis targets for aging resilience |
| Cambrian BioPharma | Compound testing | Rapamycin analogs for aging outcomes |
| Linnaeus Therapeutics | Compound testing | Cardiometabolic drugs for aging resilience |

**Where Cytognosis differs from PROSPR:**

| Dimension | PROSPR | Cytognosis | Differentiation |
| --------- | ------ | ---------- | --------------- |
| **Target population** | Adults 65+, aging-specific | All ages, cross-disorder | PROSPR is age-restricted; Cytognosis detects disease trajectories from birth onward |
| **Temporal goal** | Shorten clinical trials from decades to 3 years | Detect disease years before onset | PROSPR accelerates treatment validation; Cytognosis provides the pre-symptomatic detection layer that identifies who needs treatment |
| **Intervention vs. detection** | Therapeutic (drugs, interventions to extend healthspan) | Diagnostic infrastructure (AI models + sensors for detection) | PROSPR builds treatments; Cytognosis builds the diagnostic infrastructure that determines when treatments should begin |
| **Methodology** | Traditional clinical trials (accelerated) | Continuous population-level monitoring | PROSPR uses trial-based evidence; Cytognosis replaces episodic trials with continuous trajectories |
| **Output** | Validated aging therapeutics + "healthspan score" | Biological coordinate system (Cytoverse) + continuous sensing (Cytoscope) | Complementary: PROSPR's healthspan score could be validated against Cytoverse trajectories |
| **Data approach** | In-home digital health assessments, harmonized trial datasets | Multi-omic single-cell profiles → learned biological manifold | PROSPR collects physiological/digital signals; Cytognosis operates at the molecular/cellular layer |

**Recommended differentiation language for PHO MO ISO:**
> "Cytognosis occupies a fundamentally different layer of the proactive health stack than PROSPR. Where PROSPR develops therapeutic interventions and accelerated clinical trial protocols to validate aging treatments in adults 65+, Cytognosis builds the disease-agnostic diagnostic infrastructure, a continuous biological coordinate system trained on multi-omic data, that detects cellular disease signatures years before symptoms manifest across all age groups and disease categories. The Cytoverse health map identifies *who* needs intervention; programs like PROSPR provide *what* intervention to give them. These layers are complementary: PROSPR's healthspan score could serve as one coordinate within the Cytoverse manifold, and Cytoscope continuous sensing could feed real-time biomarker data into PROSPR's accelerated trial protocols."

### EVIDENT — **MEDIUM-HIGH OVERLAP RISK** ⚡

**Full name:** Evidence-Based Validation and Innovation for Rapid Therapeutics in Behavioral Health
**Announced:** November 2025 | **Investment:** Up to $100M | **Awards:** $4M each, 24-month, milestone-based
**Office:** PHO

**EVIDENT scope:** Catalyze a new era in behavioral health by generating and validating objective, FDA-ready clinical endpoints for emerging rapid-acting therapies (neuroplastogens, neuromodulation, digital therapeutics). Establish a Rapid Response Data Repository.

**EVIDENT Technical Areas (detailed):**

| TA | Name | Scope | Temporal Scale |
| -- | ---- | ----- | -------------- |
| TA1 | Objective Measurement of Clinical Change | Biological, digital, physiological biomarkers (neuroimaging, digital phenotyping) to monitor clinical changes | **Hours to days** |
| TA2 | Mechanisms of Rapid In-Session Change | Capture in-session data (sensors, EEG, behavioral analytics) to discover real-time predictors of clinical change and neural indicators of rapid effects | **Minutes to hours** |
| TA3 | Personal Risk and Durability Profiles | Integration of clinical, genetic, biomarker data → predictive models for risk of adverse events and treatment durability | **3 to 12 months** |
| TA4 | Supplemental Data Collection | Supporting biomarker and biological sample collection in ongoing trials of rapid-acting treatments | **Ongoing** |

**Where Cytognosis differs from EVIDENT:**

| Dimension | EVIDENT | Cytognosis | Differentiation |
| --------- | ------- | ---------- | --------------- |
| **Disease scope** | Behavioral health only (depression, PTSD, anxiety, SUD) | Disease-agnostic (neuropsych is one of many domains) | EVIDENT is locked to behavioral conditions; Cytognosis uses the same platform for metabolic, cardiovascular, oncological, and neuropsychiatric trajectories |
| **Temporal focus** | Treatment *response* (hours to months after therapy) | Disease *onset* (years before symptoms) | EVIDENT measures "did the therapy work?"; Cytognosis answers "will this person develop disease?" |
| **Biomarker purpose** | Validate existing therapies / get FDA endpoints | Discover pre-symptomatic signatures / build health coordinate system | EVIDENT uses biomarkers for regulatory approval; Cytognosis uses them for early detection |
| **Intervention layer** | Uses existing interventions (neuroplastogens, neuromodulation) | Creates the detection layer that identifies who needs intervention | EVIDENT assumes a therapy exists and measures response; Cytognosis identifies the population that needs therapy before diagnosis |
| **Data approach** | In-session sensors, EEG, behavioral analytics, neuroimaging | Multi-omic single-cell profiles, continuous multiplexed biomarker sensing | Different omics layers and modalities |
| **Population** | Clinical trial participants receiving behavioral health treatments | General population, pre-symptomatic | EVIDENT studies people already in treatment; Cytognosis monitors people who appear healthy |

**Recommended differentiation language for PHO MO ISO:**
> "EVIDENT and Cytognosis operate at different temporal points in the behavioral health trajectory. EVIDENT validates objective endpoints for rapid-acting therapies by measuring clinical change on the scale of hours to months *after treatment begins*. Cytognosis detects the biological trajectory drift toward neuropsychiatric disease *years before clinical diagnosis*, using continuous multi-omic sensing rather than in-session clinical measurements. Where EVIDENT validates 'did the therapy work?', Cytognosis answers 'who will need therapy?' The Cytoscope-Cytonome sensing platform could feed continuous pre-clinical data into EVIDENT's Rapid Response Data Repository, providing the upstream detection layer that identifies treatment candidates for the rapid-acting therapies EVIDENT is validating."

### OCULAB — **HIGH OVERLAP RISK** ⚠️

**Full name:** Ocular Laboratory for Analysis of Biomarkers
**PM:** Calvin Roberts, M.D. | **Office:** PHO

**OCULAB focus:** Continuous tear-based biomarker monitoring via nanosensor in tear duct. First target: dry eye disease. Platform potential for hormones, vitamins, proteins, inflammatory markers.

**Where Cytognosis differs:**

- OCULAB is **single-modality** (tear-based sensing only); Cytognosis is **multimodal** (blood-based + multi-omics AI)
- OCULAB targets **treatment monitoring** (closed-loop therapeutic dosing); Cytognosis targets **pre-symptomatic detection**
- OCULAB develops **hardware** (nanosensor implant); Cytognosis develops **AI/computational platform** primarily

**Recommended differentiation language:**
> "While OCULAB develops a tear-based nanosensor for continuous monitoring of known biomarkers, Cytognosis develops the computational layer that *discovers* which cellular features predict disease onset, then validates them across noninvasive modalities. Our AI platform is biomarker-agnostic and could inform what OCULAB-like sensors should measure."

### BDF Toolbox — **MEDIUM OVERLAP RISK** ⚡

**Full name:** ARPA-H Biomedical Data Fabric Toolbox
**PM:** Erika Kim, Ph.D. | **Office:** HSF

**BDF focus:** Infrastructure for integrating and sharing biomedical data across institutions.

**Where Cytognosis differs:**

- BDF is **data infrastructure** (plumbing for sharing existing datasets)
- Cytognosis is **analytical platform** (generating new insights from cellular features)
- Different layer of the stack: complementary, not competitive
- Cytoverse could be a *consumer* of BDF-integrated data, and Cytoscope data could flow *into* BDF infrastructure

### RAPID — **LOW OVERLAP RISK**

**Full name:** Rare Disease AI/ML for Precision Integrated Diagnostics
**PM:** Scott Gorman, J.D., MBA | **Office:** HSF

**RAPID focus:** AI/ML for rare disease diagnostics using phenotype-driven, genomics-heavy approaches.

**Where Cytognosis differs:**

- RAPID: **rare diseases** specifically, phenotype → genotype diagnostic pathway
- Cytognosis: **common diseases** primarily, continuous biological trajectories across all conditions
- RAPID diagnoses *existing* rare disease patients; Cytognosis monitors *healthy* populations for trajectory deviation
- Methodologically different: RAPID is genomics-heavy variant interpretation; Cytognosis is multi-omic manifold learning
