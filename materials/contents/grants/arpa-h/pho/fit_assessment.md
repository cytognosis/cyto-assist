## Expanded Focus Areas for LLM Consumption

The following expands the tailored focus areas into comprehensive natural-language descriptions that an LLM can use directly when drafting proposal sections. Each paragraph preserves ISO terminology while connecting it to Cytognosis capabilities.

### Primary Target: Continuous Health-State Sensing at Population Scale

The PHO ISO seeks "methods for continuous and widespread sensing of health-state, and early disease indicators that can be deployed at population-scales." Cytognosis addresses this through the integrated Cytoverse-Cytoscope system. Cytoverse (The Map) constructs a biological coordinate system that represents individual health states as continuous positions in a learned manifold, trained on multi-omic profiles from single-cell sequencing, proteomics, and metabolomics data. Instead of binary disease/healthy classifications that the current ICD-code system imposes, Cytoverse maps health as a continuous trajectory, making "pre-disease states" and "early disease indicators" detectable as trajectory deviations years before clinical symptoms manifest.

Cytoscope (The Sensor) provides the "continuous and widespread sensing" capability through programmable, continuous biosensing hardware. Designed for population-scale deployment, Cytoscope targets "low/no-cost sensing modalities" by leveraging advances in MEMS-based electrochemical sensors and multiplexed aptamer panels that measure circulating biomarkers continuously. The on-device Cytonome (The Navigator) runs causal AI inference at less than 5 milliwatts, enabling real-time health-state estimation without cloud connectivity, a critical enabler for deployment in underserved communities where cellular infrastructure is unreliable. This satisfies the PHO interest in solutions that are "both inexpensive and effective."

### Secondary Target: Health Span Extension and Pre-Disease State Detection

The PHO ISO highlights interest in "novel approaches to increasing individual health spans and independence even in the absence of disease" and "early indicators of both disease- and pre-disease states." Cytognosis directly addresses health span extension through proactive health monitoring. By replacing episodic clinical snapshots with continuous biological trajectory tracking, Cytognosis detects the gradual drift from healthy coordinates toward disease attractors in the Cytoverse manifold. This drift, invisible to conventional point-of-care testing, represents the "pre-disease state" that the PHO seeks to identify.

The continuous nature of the Cytoverse coordinate system means that "proactive health outcomes," those achieved before any clinical diagnosis, become measurable and quantifiable. Individuals receive actionable insights about their biological trajectory, enabling lifestyle, nutritional, or early pharmacological interventions that maintain health span without requiring a disease diagnosis first. This aligns with the PHO mandate to find "measures associated with proactive health outcomes that are both inexpensive and effective."

### IP and Standards Alignment

ARPA-H "strongly encourages IP rights to be aligned with open-source rules" and prefers "technical solutions that contain software elements with commercial-friendly open-source licenses (e.g., Massachusetts Institute of Technology, Berkley Software Distribution, or Apache 2.0)." Cytognosis Foundation's entire software stack is released under Apache 2.0, with data and publications under CC BY 4.0. This is not a concession to funder requirements but the foundation's core operating principle: precision health as a public good.

All health data components will adhere to FHIR for clinical data exchange, TEFCA for trusted network interoperability, and the Global Alliance for Genomics and Health (GA4GH) standards for genomic data. The Cytoverse data model will be developed as an open, consensus-based standard with documentation suitable for ONC evaluation, directly satisfying the ISO requirement to "develop a general-purpose open data model and to prototype new open APIs."

### Disease-Agnostic Platform Design

The ISO encourages "approaches that are disease agnostic." The Cytognosis platform is fundamentally disease-agnostic: the Cytoverse health coordinate system works across all biological domains because it learns from the underlying cellular and molecular state space rather than disease-specific features. A cancer trajectory deviation in Cytoverse uses the same coordinate system as a cardiovascular or neurodegenerative deviation. This platform approach directly mirrors what PHO describes as the need for "novel, robust, and predictive surrogates for long-term health outcomes with associated epidemiological models."

## Portfolio Differentiation (Mandatory for MO ISO Submission)

ARPA-H requires proposers to review the full program portfolio and demonstrate that their idea falls outside the scope of existing research. The following programs/initiatives have overlap with Cytognosis and require explicit differentiation in the PHO MO ISO proposal.

### vs. PROSPR (HIGH overlap, PHO)

PROSPR develops therapeutic interventions and accelerated clinical trial protocols (3-year trials instead of decades) to validate aging treatments in adults 65+. Cytognosis operates at a different layer:

| Dimension | PROSPR | Cytognosis |
| --------- | ------ | ---------- |
| Population | Adults 65+, aging-specific | All ages, cross-disorder |
| Goal | Shorten trials, validate treatments | Detect disease years before onset |
| Layer | Therapeutic (drugs to extend healthspan) | Diagnostic (AI + sensors for detection) |
| Method | Accelerated clinical trials | Continuous population-level monitoring |
| Output | Validated aging drugs + "healthspan score" | Cytoverse coordinate system + Cytoscope sensing |

**Use in proposal:** "Cytognosis builds the disease-agnostic diagnostic infrastructure layer that identifies *who* needs intervention; programs like PROSPR provide *what* intervention to give them. PROSPR's healthspan score could serve as one coordinate within the Cytoverse manifold."

### vs. EVIDENT (MEDIUM-HIGH overlap, PHO)

EVIDENT validates objective clinical endpoints for rapid-acting behavioral health therapies (hours-to-months after treatment). Cytognosis operates upstream:

| Dimension | EVIDENT | Cytognosis |
| --------- | ------- | ---------- |
| Scope | Behavioral health only | Disease-agnostic |
| Temporal | Treatment response (hours-months) | Disease onset (years before symptoms) |
| Question | "Did the therapy work?" | "Who will need therapy?" |
| Population | Trial participants in treatment | General population, pre-symptomatic |
| Biomarker purpose | Regulatory validation | Early detection |

**Use in proposal:** "EVIDENT validates therapeutic endpoints; Cytognosis provides the upstream detection layer that identifies treatment candidates before clinical diagnosis. Cytoscope data could feed into EVIDENT's Rapid Response Data Repository."

### vs. OCULAB (HIGH overlap, PHO)

OCULAB develops single-modality tear-based nanosensors for treatment monitoring.

**Use in proposal:** "Cytognosis develops the computational layer that discovers which cellular features predict disease onset, then validates them across modalities. Our platform is biomarker-agnostic and could inform what OCULAB-like sensors should measure."
