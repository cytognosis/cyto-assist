## How to Use This Matrix

1. **Before writing**: Identify which criteria the target funder scores — focus 80% of effort there
2. **Cross-pollination**: Answers written for one funder's criteria can be reused/adapted for others
3. **Shared question groups** (write once, adapt many):
   - **Problem/Gap/Innovation** → ARPA-H (Concept Summary), YC (What does your company make?), Foresight (What are you trying to do?), Heilmeier Q1-Q3
   - **Team** → ARPA-H (Team Organization), YC (founders), Convergent (Team & Timeline)
   - **Impact/Who Cares** → ARPA-H (Innovation and Impact), Heilmeier Q4-Q5, EA (Project Goal)
   - **Why FRO/Why Not Startup** → Convergent (Why a FRO is Essential), NSF Tech Labs, Heilmeier additional Q
   - **Openness** → NSF Tech Labs, Convergent (Open Science Commitment), Foresight, Astera
